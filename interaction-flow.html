Interaction FLow

Precondition: Angela is logged into Temu on iPhone.

Postcondition: Angela adds an item to her cart.

  • Angela clicks on Temu app on her iPhone.
  • The app opens up to Temu homepage.
  • Angela types 'Mini' into the search bar on the top of the page
  • The page loads to a grid view of the available miniature items.
  • Angela sees an item she likes and taps on the cart icon.
  • The cart icon on that specific item now has an orange '1' on the top of it.
  • Item is now added to cart.
