Site: Temu

Persona: Angela

Tech: Angela has an iPhone, and not really much else. She's an average tech user.

Love/Hate: Angela loves endlessly scrolling through social media and sites like Amazon and Temu. She hates when a new app freezes and when it starts acting buggy when she's been on it for a while.

Proficiency: Angela is an average user, so she knows how to do the basics such as going into her settings, launching apps, downloading apps, deleting apps, and following simple in-app navigation.

Attitudes: After a long day at work, she likes to relax and scroll for a few hours. Looking at funny videos or new knick knacks from Temu.

Needs: She needs an easy-to-use app that doesn't randomly quit.

Frustrations: Angela gets upset when she can't easily navigate through an app or a website.
